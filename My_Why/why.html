My Why

This is third time I have tried to get onto this course. The first time was for the 2020 summer cohort. As soon as I found out about the possibility of joining this community, I started trying to fulfil all the necessary prerequisites. After a while I realised that I didn’t have enough time to finish everything, absorb all information and be ready to apply, so I decided to give myself some extra time to digest everything and wait until the winter cohort.

Before the winter application opening, I had almost finished all my prerequisites. I then learned, suddenly, that the winter cohort had been cancelled. I would be lying if I was to say that I didn’t feel sad. Of course, I did, but life didn’t stop, and I still had hope. I didn’t stop learning and now I'm applying for this spring cohort. I really hope that the third time I’ll be lucky, and I will be accepted onto this course. Fingers crossed.

I came to this country 2 years ago; not to find a better life, but to be with my partner. I left my family, close friends and a job that I loved, with the hope to start a new life here. When I arrived, I felt like a small girl in the big, big world: no friends, no education (my degree here had no value and I didn't want to work in management) and virtually no language. I had to start everything from scratch, and I lacked confidence. I understood that I had two choices: sit at home and cry or do something about it. I decided to move forward. I learned the language, finished a social care course with the hope to start my first job in my new home, but COVID-19 changed all my plans. So, I needed to find another way to realise myself, to create, and be useful to society.

Deep inside I always wanted to be part of a community, to do something important. It was by accident that I was introduced to programing, but I truly believe that there are no real accidents in life. Everything comes at the right time.

So, now I'm writing to you through my own built website (that I’m very proud of), with a big will to go forward, to dive deeper in the ocean of knowledge. I know that here in FAC I will find (and have already found) an incredible community of amazing people and I know that together we can create something mind-blowing what will change the world.

Everything begins from a dream...
